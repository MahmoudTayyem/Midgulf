Midgulf International Group is an international trading house with extensive expertise in the sourcing, origination and marketing of fertilizers and related raw materials across the world. Established in 1976, today we remain a family business with headquarters in Cyprus and strategically located offices across the world.

With an exceptional reputation built over 40 years, today, Midgulf is established as one of the most trusted trading houses in the world. The integrity and transparency that underpins every negotiation is matched only by the flawless execution of each contract. With an infrastructure designed to provide a local presence in many key locations across the world, Midgulf are in prime position to manage vast volumes of product from source to destination.
